Privacy Policy

Husky Enhanced Audio Remover

Effective date: August 09, 2025

Overview

Husky Enhanced Audio Remover (“the App”) removes audio tracks from video files on your Mac. The App performs all processing locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App:

  • Does not create user accounts.
  • Does not connect to external servers or third‑party SDKs.
  • Does not track usage analytics or crash metrics.
  • Does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, calendars, or location.

Files & Permissions

The App requires permission to read the video files you select and to write the processed (audio‑free) videos to a folder you choose. These files remain on your device and are not uploaded.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Security

Because all processing happens locally and no data is transmitted, exposure to network risks is minimized. You are responsible for safeguarding your device and backups.
